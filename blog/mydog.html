Olly - My Dog

By Sigton, 17/12/16

I have a dog. He's a lovely dog. His name is Olly. And he is so adorable! Olly is just over a year old, and we got him last Christmas. He is a golden retriever, though he hasn't really got the hang of retrieving things yet. He'll run after the ball, pick it up, start running back, then drop it half way. Maybe one day he'll learn, who knows.
