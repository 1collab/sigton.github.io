Fall Ninja 2

Instructions and Description

Fall Ninja is a game of reactions, are you quick enough to win?

I actually made this game from scratch in less than 48 hours! That's what happens when I've got nothing better to do in the holidays... I spent most of the time working on the graphics, I think it looks really neat.

How to Play

All you do is push space to jump across the chasm. You need to make sure to dodge the spikes!
