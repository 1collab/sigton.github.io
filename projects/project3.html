3D Flying Simulator

Instructions and Description

Controls: W/S - Pitch (vertical) control
A/D - Yaw (horizontal) control
Left/right arrows - Roll control
Up/down arrows - Thrust control
R - Reset
The 'T' stands for thrust.
Space starts at altitude 10,000

Music is To The Stars by Braken.
