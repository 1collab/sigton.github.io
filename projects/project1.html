Reflections

Instructions and Description

Reflections is an original platforming game, which takes the idea of reflection and applies it to tricky and inginuitive levels designed to force you to think and have to look at both sides of the screen!

Controls:
Left/right arrows or A/D to walk left/right.
Up arrow, W or space bar to jump.
Click to attack.

If there are any problems, restart the project and they should go away. I recommend double-clicking the green flag just in case.
